🌿 Self-Care Resources

4-7-8 Breathing

Breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat 4 times.

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer break.

Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)

Acknowledge 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, and 1 you taste.
